The Center of the

"Circular Cities"

Because Minneapolis/St. Paul is the global hub for enterprise IT asset reuse and refurbishment, Alta is a great place to send your excess IT assets.

In the 1980s, a large IBM refurbishers, Data 3, imploded and spawned multiple refurbishers, resulting in a Minnesota ecosystem of scores of recyclers in refurbishers here.

Today, after 30 years in business, Alta continues to be the global leader, operating out of this unique epicenter of data center resale, serving thousands of accounts worldwide.
