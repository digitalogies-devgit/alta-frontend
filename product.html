Lenovo RS160 Specifications

Form factor: 1U rack-mount

Processor: Intel Xeon processor E3-1200 v5 product family with four cores up to 3.7 GHz, 8 MB cache, and up to 2133 MHz memory speed; or Intel Core i3 processor 6100/6300 product families with two cores up to 3.9 GHz, up to 4 MB cache, and 2133 MHz memory speed; or Intel Pentium processor G4400/G4500 product families with two cores up to 3.6 GHz, 3 MB cache, and up to 2133 MHz memory speed.

Chipset: Intel C232

Memory: Four DIMM sockets. Support for ECC UDIMMs. DIMM speeds up to 2133 MHz.

Memory Capacity: Up to 64 GB with four 16 GB UDIMMs

Power Supply: One fixed 300 W AC (100 - 240 V) power supply (80 PLUS Gold)

Optical Drive Bays: One. Support for DVD-ROM or DVD-RW

I/O Expansion Slots: Up to one PCIe 3.0 x16 (x16 wired) low-profile slot with an optional riser card

Dimensions: Height: 43 mm (1.7 in.), Width: 439 mm (17.3 in.), Depth: 411 mm (16.2 in.)

Weight: Minimum: 6.2 kg (13.7 lb); Maximum: 7.6 kg (16.8 lb)
